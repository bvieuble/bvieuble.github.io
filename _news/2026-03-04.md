I am delighted to organize with [Xiaobo Liu](https://xiaobo-liu.github.io/) the minisymposium “Mixed Precision Algorithms: A Rounding Error Analysis Perspective” for the SIAM PP26 conference in Berlin this year. Our speakers are: Erin Carson (Charles University), Tom Caruso (Sorbonne University), Xiaobo Liu (MPI), Xinye Chen (Sorbonne Université), Yida Li (China University of Petroleum), Clément Peaucelle (Airbus), and Juan Zhang (Xiangtan University).
